3. The Core Challenges & Why They Matter

Challenge #1: The Operational "Leaky Bucket"

The biggest issue is that **61% of the calls you are paying for are being missed**. We are spending money to fill a bucket with leads, but most are leaking out. Those 59 missed calls represent 59 lost opportunities, which could have translated to an additional **15 lost customers** and **₹37,500 in lost revenue**.

Challenge #2: The Sales Conversion Rate

The current data shows that of the 38 calls you answered, 10 became customers. This is a **sales conversion rate of 26%**. While this is a good start, improving this rate is key to increasing profitability and allowing us to scale the budget more aggressively and achieve your goals faster.

4. The Path to 50 Lakhs: A Realistic, Phased Plan

Your goal of ~334 clients per month is ambitious, but achievable over time by fixing the foundational issues and scaling intelligently. Here is our proposed plan.

Phase 1: Fix the Foundation (Next 30-60 Days)

Our immediate goal is to make the campaign highly profitable by fixing the leaks. My role is to optimize ad spend to reduce the Cost Per Lead (cost per >20s call) from its current ₹940 to under ₹500. Your team's role is to increase the Call Answer Rate to over 90% and improve the sales conversion rate.

Phase 2: Profitable Growth (Months 3-6)

Once the system is efficient, we can confidently scale. By increasing the budget to **₹50,000 per month**, we can aim to generate approximately **100 high-quality leads** (calls > 20s). This would translate to 30-40 new customers per month and revenue of ₹75,000 - ₹1,00,000.

Phase 3: Aggressive Scaling (Months 7-15)

To reach your target of 334+ clients per month, we will need to further scale the budget towards the **₹1.5 Lakh - ₹2 Lakh per month** range, but only after we have proven the model is highly profitable and your team is ready for the high volume of calls.

5. Summary of Responsibilities: Our Partnership

Achieving this ambitious target requires a partnership where we each focus on our area of expertise.

My Responsibility (Performance Marketer)

  • Generate a high volume of relevant phone calls at the lowest possible cost.
  • Optimize ads and keywords to continuously improve lead quality.
  • Provide transparent reports to make data-driven decisions together.

Your Responsibility (The Business)

  • Ensure **every single phone call is answered** during business hours.
  • Effectively **convert the leads** we generate into paying customers.
  • Provide feedback on lead quality to help refine ad targeting.
